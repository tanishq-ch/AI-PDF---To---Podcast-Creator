Revolutionizing Content with Voice

Welcome to Podcast AI, where your documents learn to speak.

We believe in making information more accessible, engaging, and human. Our mission is to empower creators, educators, and businesses by transforming static text into dynamic, high-quality audio experiences that can be enjoyed anywhere.

Our Process: From Text to Voice

1. Content Ingestion

You begin by securely uploading a document (PDF or TXT). Our system intelligently extracts the core text, preparing it for transformation.

2. AI Script Generation

The text is passed to a Large Language Model, which reimagines it as a natural, conversational script between multiple speakers.

3. Lifelike Voice Synthesis

Our Text-to-Speech (TTS) engine renders the script into lifelike voices, creating a polished, ready-to-share audio file.

Our Vision for the Future

This project showcases the potential of modern AI to foster a more personal connection with digital content. We envision a future where any piece of writing can be experienced as a conversation, making learning and information consumption more enjoyable and efficient for everyone.
